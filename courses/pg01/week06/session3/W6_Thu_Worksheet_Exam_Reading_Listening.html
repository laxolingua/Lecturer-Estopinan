Part 1 — Reading

Text: "A beginner's guide to sustainable farming" (UNEP, 2024). Read individually first.

As you read, watch for four threads

Environment  ·  Cost  ·  Health  ·  Diet & access

Comprehension questions (answer in your own words)

  1. Do you think this is a credible source? Why? (Apply the CRAAP test from Wednesday.)
  2. What is the purpose of this article?
  3. What are the Sustainable Development Goals and the Zero Hunger initiative?
  4. How does sustainable agriculture make a difference to the environment?
  5. What makes sustainably produced food more expensive?
  6. What is the impact on health of chemicals, medicine and genetic modification?
  7. What does the article say about veganism?
  8. What are the economic benefits of sustainable agriculture in developing countries?
  9. What can make sustainably produced food affordable to everyone?
  10. What are the two main reasons for the lack of sustainable farming worldwide?
Pair discussion: compare your answers. Where you disagree, point to the sentence in the text that supports your reading.
