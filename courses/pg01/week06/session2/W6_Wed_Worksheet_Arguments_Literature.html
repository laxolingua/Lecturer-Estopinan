Self-check before you stop:
  • Is my claim a single, clear point?
  • Is my evidence sourced and cited?
  • Have I actually written the warrant (not assumed it)?
  • Did I name a real objection, refute it, and reinforce?
  • Did I hedge any contested evidence (may, suggests, tends to…)?
