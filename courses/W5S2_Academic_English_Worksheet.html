Self-Editing Checklist — Assessment 1

Before submitting your report, go through this checklist sentence by sentence.

  • Have I used correct punctuation throughout? (no contractions, capital letters where needed, correct commas)
  • Have I used linking words to connect my ideas logically?
  • Have I hedged any claims that could be challenged? (may, tends to, appears to…)
  • Have I removed all personal pronouns (I, we, you) except where field conventions allow?
  • Have I used impersonal structures? (It is argued that… / The data suggests…)
